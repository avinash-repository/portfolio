About Me

I am a dedicated Data Analyst with a strong foundation in computer science and a passion for transforming data into actionable insights. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science from the National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra, I have developed a range of technical skills, including Python, Power BI, SQL, and Tableau, to drive data analysis projects effectively..

From analyzing sentiment in product reviews to tracking housing market trends and examining job market dynamics, I have applied tools such as BeautifulSoup, TextBlob, and Power Query to extract, clean, and visualize data. Additionally, my experience as a Data Analyst intern at Suvidha Foundation enhanced my proficiency in using Excel and Power BI to streamline reporting processes, improve donor retention, and support strategic decision-making.

With a blend of technical expertise and hands-on project experience, I am enthusiastic about helping organizations leverage data for meaningful outcomes. I look forward to connecting with like-minded professionals and contributing to data-driven projects that make an impact.
